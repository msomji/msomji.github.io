Fireside chat with Shareef

December 14, 2014

There are multiple different types of personalities on the internet to allow one to be able to pin point what kind of thinking style they prefer and how this affects their other activities. Professor Anthony Gregorc, a professor of curriculum and instruction at the University of Connecticut had identified that there are four separate types of preferred thinking type.

  • Concrete Sequential thinking style:

    Such individuals tend to be very logical thinkers and depend mainly on a logical and ordered process.

  • Concrete Random Thinkers:

    Such thinkers enjoy experimenting what their tasks to reach the desired destination.

  • Abstract Random thinkers

    Such random thinker are more so visual reflectors but strive in an unstructured environment

  • Abstract sequential thinkers

    These individuals depend on theories and usually use abstract ideas to complete their projects.

It is important to remember that none of the types of thinkers are better than their counter parts as each just uses a different method to solve the task at hand.

After completing the test I received a score that placed me as a mainly abstract random thinker. This came to me as a surprise as I would have thought I was more of a concrete sequential thinker.

You should try it for your self.
