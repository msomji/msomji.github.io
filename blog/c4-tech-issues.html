We have a Bug Problem

January 18, 2015

Nothing in this world is perfect but the least we can do is to strive for the best. Like in any workplace, there is a lot of space for improvement. One of the most significant problems noticed in the tech world is the drastic men to women ratio at the workplace. As of 2014, about 70% of apple employees were male (Hawver, 2014). Though this imbalance between the sexes has been an ongoing discussion in most workplaces, it is more prominent in the tech world.

Though the imbalance has been improved over the years, I believe that we are on the right path to the solution. By advertising more jobs directed towards women and also incentivizing companies to maintain a balanced ratio between the two sexes will slowly but eventually allow the general public to reach the goal.
